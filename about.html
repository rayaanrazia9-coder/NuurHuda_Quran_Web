About This Website

This website is created to help Muslims around the world easily read and listen to the Holy Quran anytime and anywhere. The goal of this platform is to make the Quran accessible through a simple and clean digital experience.

The website includes the complete Quran with all 114 Surahs and the full 30 Juz structure. Users can easily navigate between Surahs and Ayahs and listen to beautiful Quran recitations.

It also provides reminders that encourage Muslims to read and reflect on the Quran daily. The website is designed to work smoothly on both mobile phones and computers.

Website Features

  • 114 Complete Surahs
  • 30 Juz Navigation
  • Audio Quran Recitation
  • Ayah Reminder
  • Search Surah or Ayah
  • Simple and clean design
