About NuurHuda

A simple, clean way to read and listen to the Qur’an.

This website helps Muslims around the world read and listen to the Holy Qur’an anytime and anywhere. The goal is to make the Qur’an accessible through a focused, calm, and modern digital experience.

The site includes all 114 Surahs and full 30 Juz navigation. You can browse Surahs and Ayahs, and listen to beautiful recitations.

It also offers gentle reminders to read and reflect daily. The layout adapts to phones and desktops for a smooth experience.

Features

  • 114 Surahs with clear navigation
  • 30 Juz browsing
  • Audio recitation support
  • Daily reminder motivation
  • Simple, readable design
