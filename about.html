About NuurHuda

Your companion on your Quran journey 🌟

Nuurhuda Quran is a digital platform designed to help Muslims stay consistent with Quran reading 📖. We are focused on building a strong daily connection with the Quran, providing a calm and modern experience 🤍.

Our mission is to make the Holy Quran accessible anytime and anywhere, ensuring that every user can engage with the divine words in a meaningful way.

Nuurhuda is more than just an app — it’s a dedicated space for reflection, growth, and spiritual consistency.

Why NuurHuda?

  • Smart Scheduling: Plan your reading journey with our 30-day Juz plan ⚙️
  • Progress Tracking: Visualize your achievements with interactive progress bars and stats 📊
  • Daily Motivation: Stay inspired with personalized reminders and congratulatory messages 🎉
  • Full Content: Easy access to all 114 Surahs and 30 Juz 📖
  • Audio Experience: Listen to beautiful recitations for better reflection 🎧

Built with Passion

Nuurhuda is built by a passionate team of developers 👨‍💻 who are committed to creating tools that serve the Ummah and foster a deeper love for the Quran.
